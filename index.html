Meet Our Doctor

Dr. Mansour is the founder and lead physician at Mansour Medical, with over 10 years of experience in pain management. He specializes in the latest non-surgical, sterile and safe treatments using orthobiologic therapies with ultrasound-guided imaging along with interstitial fiber-optic laser and ozone therapy to further improve clinical outcomes for treating pain/inflammation. These orthobiologics are sterile, FDA pre-screened allografts from approved placental tissue-derived/mesenchymal stem cell donor banks that often perform better than PRP, bone-marrow or adipose-derived stem cell therapies and demonstrate less post-procedure inflammation/side effects and accelerated recovery.

Dr. Mansour is dedicated to providing personalized care to help patients regain mobility and live pain-free.

Frequently Asked Questions

I take a whole‑person approach to pain relief that considers lifestyle, environment, nutrition - going beyond just symptoms to address underlying dysfunction. My diverse toolkit includes regenerative injections, laser treatments, ozone therapy and more.

My experience spans a wide spectrum - from sports injuries and nerve pain to complex autoimmune diseases. I even treat tricky, hard‑to‑pinpoint cases like mold toxicity and Lyme disease.

I utilize several cutting‑edge treatments, like ultrasound‑guided hydrodissection to precisely inject healing substances into inflamed areas. Orthobiologics like Placental Matrix can stimulate the body’s natural repair processes. The Weber Endolaser system delivers targeted photobiomodulation to reduce inflammation and facilitate tissue regeneration. I’m also certified in ozone injections to ease joint pain through anti‑inflammatory effects (Book FREE 15 min Info Call for supporting research).

Delivering concentrated nutrients directly into the bloodstream can help modulate pain pathways and accelerate tissue repair. Customized IV formulas at the appropriate dosing for body weight/activity level with ingredients like vitamin C, amino acids, carnitine, magnesium and curcumin can reduce inflammation, support healing and ease discomfort.

I help patients implement stress‐reduction techniques, sleep strategies and autonomic nervous system/vagus nerve support using advanced stimulation techniques with Stimpod and/or the Stellate Ganglion Block. Sometimes specialised treatments like transcranial laser therapy can beneficially impact pain perception on a neurological level especially for traumatic brain injury/concussion, neurodegenerative or post-stroke conditions. Addressing emotional health by modulation of the “fight or flight”/autonomic nervous system is imperative for lasting relief.

Response times vary greatly depending on the complexity of each case. Some patients improve remarkably quickly within a session or two. More entrenched issues may require months of dedicated protocols to unlock optimal wellness. I continually track progress to tailor care based on the way each body responds and the severity of the condition.
